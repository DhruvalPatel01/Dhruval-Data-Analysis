About Me

Hi all! I am a data analyst with a passion for turning raw datasets into clear, actionable insights.

I’ve worked on diverse projects across business intelligence, marketing analytics, and healthcare, applying tools like Python, SQL, R, Tableau, Power BI, and Excel to uncover patterns, solve problems, and support data-driven decision-making.

On this GitHub, you’ll find projects where I:

  • Clean and transform real-world datasets for analysis
  • Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover trends
  • Build predictive models using regression, classification, and clustering
  • Create compelling data visualisations using Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, and Tableau
  • Translate insights into clear reports and dashboards for both technical and non-technical stakeholders

I am currently completing a Data Analytics Career Accelerator at The London School of Economics, where I applied a range of analytical techniques to tackle complex problems, from identifying healthcare service gaps to analysing marketing campaign performance.

I’m always keen to collaborate or connect — especially on projects that use data to make a difference.
